ABOUT THE COLLECTIVE

BANDO is a cycling collective born in the streets of Belo Horizonte, focused on creating content and building community around urban mobility. Founded in 2020, we bring together cyclists from all over the city through weekly rides, events and digital content.

Our mission is to document and celebrate the cycling culture while advocating for more bike-friendly urban spaces. We believe in the power of two wheels to transform cities and connect people.

What started as a small group of friends riding together has grown into a movement with thousands of members and supporters. We produce videos, photos and events that showcase the vibrant cycling scene of BH.

OUR VALUES

Community: We believe in the power of collective action.

Creativity: We express our passion through original content.

Activism: We advocate for better cycling infrastructure.
